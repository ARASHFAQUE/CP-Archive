M = 10^9 + 7

*** Modular Arithmetic Formulas ***
1. (a + b) % M = ((a % M) + (b % M))% M

2. (a * b) % M = ((a % M) * (b % M))% M

3. (a - b) % M = ((a % M) - (b % M) + M)% M (Special Case of '-')

4. (a / b) % M = ((a % M) * ((b^-1) % M))% M ** Important

** b^-1 => Multiplicative Inverse
